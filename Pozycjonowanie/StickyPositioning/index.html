Sticky positioning can be thought of as a hybrid of relative and fixed positioning. A stickily positioned element is treated as relatively positioned until it crosses a specified threshold, at which point it is treated as fixed.

For example position: sticky; top: 10px; would position an element relatively until the screen were scrolled such that the element would be less than 10 pixels from the top. Beyond that the element would be fixed to 10 pixels from the top.
